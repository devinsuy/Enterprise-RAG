Berkeley Graduate Student Profile

Concurrent degree programs have been established to provide an integrated curriculum of greater breadth between two disciplines.

For specific information and application deadlines for these programs, contact the departments or schools involved. In order to be admitted to a concurrent degree, you must be admitted to both participating programs.
