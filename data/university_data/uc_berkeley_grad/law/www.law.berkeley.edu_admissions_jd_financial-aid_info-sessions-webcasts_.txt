										How to budget around the cost of attendance
										
This session hosted by the Berkeley Law Financial Aid Office on November 17, 2022 covers strategies to minimize student loan borrowing and maximize scholarships.

View the recording

										“Loans for Law School” Webinar - June 25th, 2024 @ 11am PST
										
Join the Berkeley Law Financial Aid Office and AccessLex to learn the ins and outs of student loan borrowing. We will cover Federal, Private, and Institutional Loan borrowing options. We will also go into detail on how to determine loan borrowing amounts, strategies to keep your interest and fees to a bare minimum, and the step-by-step process of how and when to borrow loans. The webinar presentation will be followed by 15-minute 1:1 breakout sessions with an AccessLex representative for general loan questions, AccessLex resources, and other loan borrowing related questions. New and continuing students are welcome to join. We look forward to seeing you!

When:
 Jun 25, 2024 11:00 AM PST

Virtual Registration link: 
https://accesslex-org.zoom.us/webinar/register/WN_5x9OSccTQVevI5n4X7Be9g#/registration
 (opens in a new tab)

										Paul and Daisy Soros Fellowship presentation - Berkeley Law alumni
										
The Paul & Daisy Soros Fellowships for New Americans is a $90,000 fellowship that honors the immigrant spirit of creativity, diligence, hard work, and a commitment to giving back to the United States. Berkeley Law Alumni Jonah Lalas and other Paul and Daisy Soros Fellows share their experiences with the Fellowship, and provide helpful tips on a successful application.
